Facebook Business Ionic - Turn your Facebook page into a mobile app

Facebook Business Ionic uses your existing Facebook page to fetch its content and present it in a branded mobile app without the need for any data custom-created for your mobile app.

Facebook Business Ionic exposes your news feed to your clients including status updates, photos, videos, links, activity and more. Also, it allows you to display your Facebook page photo albums, events and reviews right into your mobile app to get users’ attention and keep it. This app is a perfect way to reach your audience and make useful company information, such as address, phone number and opening hours, easily accessible. It also increases customer engagement with convenient features such as adding an event to the user’s calendar and providing directions for a location.

This app works seamlessly with your Facebook page with just one step configuration. Best practices, proven techniques and experienced software architecture are packed with a well-crafted design.

In a nutshell

Facebook Business Ionic features your Facebook page content to get the most out of it and spread awareness of your business. It requires minimal setup enabling even non-developers to use and maintain it.

Features:

Business Information

Keep users informed of your business important information. Action buttons enable easy access to the business location, phone number, website and email. A convenient list of opening hours and a “Closed now”/”Open now” label gives the best overview of the business operating hours.

News

Share interesting posts and activities to give your business updates in a tidy package.

Events

Make your events stand out by displaying all the relevant information through a minimalistic and clean design. Getting directions makes finding the event location faster and easier and adding it to the device calendar engages your fans even more.

Map

Mark your location on map to help customers find your location faster without leaving the app.

Albums

Make your photo albums the cornerstone of your business. Tell your story of let your products shine with your valuable photographs accompanied by titles and descriptions.

Reviews

Earn loyalty and promote the reputation of your business showing your awesome reviews.

Easy Configuration / Modular Architecture

The configuration of this app requires literally a single step. Its modular architecture is going to eliminate any unnecessary complexity for its configuration or modification/addition of its features. Its code is clean and well-organised into modules. You could review this super-modular architecture by checking our SuperModular Ionic starter, which is provided for free.

Ionic design

This app is based on the UI elements that the Ionic Framework provides. Ionic Framework comes with a detailed and impressive CSS framework for layout styling. Moreover, Ionic comes with its own icon library built in which contains almost everything you should need. Use Ionic’s UI components and they will give you a fully customised and functional UI.
