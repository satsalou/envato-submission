Highlights

User Authentication-Management

Users are able to register, sign in and reset their password. Through the administration interface, the administrator can create accounts manually, modify or delete the registered users.

Menu Builder

Restaurant menu is built allowing highly personalised orders with multiple sizes/prices and options with or without extra cost. Out of stock items can be marked accordingly so your apps can handle them.

Deals and Promotions

Bundle your menu items to offer a meal deal to your customers. Promote an item with a reduced price or apply an exclusive discount on the total amount of the shopping cart to make your app even more appealing.

Order Management

Your orders managed in a quick and simple manner. Use this powerful backend to manage your orders and see orders history with some simple points and clicks.

Business Setup

Important information for your business such as opening hours, photos, social media accounts and address are exposed to benefit your restaurant app. Opening hours could not allow an order placement, social media content could be displayed inside your app etc.

Technologies

AppSeed Restaurant Backend is based on the MEAN stack combining the powers of MongoDB, Express, AngularJS and Node.js. These technologies enable a dynamic, fast, robust and maintainable web application.
MongoDB holds all the data of the application and Express is responsible for providing the Web API and organising the app into an MVC architecture on the server side. AngularJS is used as a client-side MVC framework that provides a dynamic UI and reduces server load. Node.js enables the maintenance of a persistent connection from the browser back to the server where both the client and the server can initiate communication and exchange data.
