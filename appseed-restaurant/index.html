AppSeed Restaurant - Full Application with self hosted backend

AppSeed Restaurant is a complete food ordering system for presenting a restaurant menu and accepting orders on the go. It comes with a powerful Self Hosted Administration Panel, where restaurant owners can create/update their menu and prices, their business information and manage their orders. This mobile app is a companion of the AppSeed Platform and provides a wealth of benefits for both restaurant clients and restaurant owners. Note: You will need to create a free account on the AppSeed Platform in order to use the mobile application. The mobile app talks to the AppSeed Platform.

Mobile App

Access to Menu



Display your menu to your customers with images, text description, prices and ingredients list. Also, display and cross-sell the extra options that can accompany a menu item.

Access to Offers



Users can locate your offers easily and add them to their cart, for ordering.

Business Presentation

Important information about your restaurant such as opening hours, location on map, contact details and social networks will be available and easily accessible by your clients.

Full Mobile Ordering Capability

Customers can place orders through a quick and simple process. They can customize their order items choosing between several ingredient options, and submit a comment for a particular product and/or the whole order. Orders are allowed only during the opening hours. Otherwise, users are notified with a friendly, customizable message.

Multiple Delivery Types



Customers can opt for home delivery, take away or dine in.

Automatic Content Update

All the content of the mobile app is synchronized with the content of the administration panel without any additional configuration.

Order History



Mobile users have access to their current and previous orders. They can view them and stay updated with their status. The status of an order is automatically updated according to the AppSeed Platform.

Restaurant Backend / Appseed Platform

Self hosted control panel with an incredible free-for-life tier and an intuitive user interface: http://my.appseed.io

Menu Builder - Menu Digitalization



Create menu categories and menu items with images, description, price and ingredient basic options at no cost for the customers as well extra ingredient options with additional cost for the customers.

Orders Control Panel



Restaurant staff can view incoming orders in real time. An order status can be updated to In progress, Cancelled, Shipped, Completed etc. Also, a distinct sound notifies the staff for newly incoming and/or unread orders.

Customer Notifications

Your customers are notified by email on each order status change.

Order Printing

An order can be printed to any desktop or thermal receipt printer according to the restaurant's operations.

Store Management

Manage business info, opening hours, location, social media accounts and contact info.

Promotions

Promote offers and special menu items. Activate or deactivate them whenever you want. Also, set a discount to be applied on the total amount of the user's shopping cart.

Browser Compatibility

AppSeed Platform is designed to be functional on all major web browsers.

Devices

The code is extremely tested on iOS and Android mobile phones.

Expand your technologies using AppSeed's RESTful API

Expand the reach of your business technologies using Appseed's RESTful API! The AppSeed Platform comes with a well-documented Restful JSON API that handles authentication and access to all the data that resides in the application. A single step is required in order to create an API user through the platform's interface and you are all set to make API calls.
